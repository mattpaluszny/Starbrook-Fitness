Starbrook Fitness

Personal Training
in a state of the art, home facility

Strength training has changed my life... and it will change yours, too! My mission is to build a fitness "career" as a way of improving upon my own health transformation. To encourage women over 40 to develop life-long habits for better health. To teach women the ways and techniques to achieve their fitness goals. To inspire and offer hope to women who, like me, struggle with weight loss/management.

Why Workout?

Strength training is one of the best things a woman over 40 can do for her body, mind & spirit

  • Get in shape faster
  • Enhance muscle tone
  • Prevent muscle loss
  • Strengthen bones
  • Feel more energized
  • Boost your metabolism
  • Reduce stress
  • Sleep better
  • Improve cognitive function
  • Increase joint flexibility
  • Prevent cardiovascular disease
  • Be happier

My Story

I've struggled with my weight for decades, but now I know that long-term success isn't possible without strength training. I've shed 50 pounds, defied a family history of obesity, and reversed a pre-diabetes diagnosis. There's no doubt that I'm loving my new way of life. I've come a long way and I'm still going strong! So, if you are ready to add strength training to your life, I'd love to help you achieve your fitness goals! I am a Certified Personal Trainer by the American Council on Exercise (ACE), have specialty certification in senior fitness, educational focus on weight management, nutrition and Integrated Fitness Training (IFT). I, also, am a 2nd-degree blackbelt student, certified in CPR/AED and First-aid by the American Heart Association.
